About me

I am a postdoc at Maryland University, College Park. My mentor is Prof. Tamás Darvas. Before that, I obtained my PhD in Tsinghua University in Beijing under the supervision of Prof. Akito Futaki. I am interested in Kähler geometry, especially existence of canonical Kähler metrics.
